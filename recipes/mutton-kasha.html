Mutton Kasha

Traditional Mutton Kasha with little water

This deliclious food is prepared by mutton. Young goats are best for this dish. Rice,mutton kasha with a litle bit of onionn, lemon, chilli, water is a classic. Suited for lunch.

This delicate meal is judged by how much oily is you hand after finishing the food. There by determinnig the qality of the meat!!

Ingredients

  • Mutton
  • Onion
  • Chilli
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Meat Masala
  • Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Wash your mutton clenaly
  2. Pour some salt,turmeric powder,chilli powder,meat masala
  3. Marinate it for half-an hour
  4. Pour oil in a karai and heat it
  5. Pour the meat in karai after oil is hot
  6. After half an hour pour potato,onion,tomaato
  7. Fry them in hot oil untill they are brownish
  8. Enjoy!!
