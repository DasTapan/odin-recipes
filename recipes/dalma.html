Dalma

This dish is a veg only item. It is enjoyed by serving coconaut cream,chilli powder on it. This can be enjoyed 365 days but it is specail on Thursday,Sankaranti.

People add home made chilli powder and enjoy it with ghee rice. It should neither watery nor too viscous.

Ingredients

  • Dal
  • Bringer
  • Potato
  • Ginger
  • Chilli
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Vegetables
  • Ghee
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Take water in a pot
  2. Place it on gas and heat
  3. Pour dal,turmeric powder,salt
  4. Pour vegetables
  5. Cook it untill dal gets to semi liquid state
  6. Take a small plate, put some ghee,phutan,jira
  7. Heat it and pour them inside the pot and cover it
  8. Enjoy!!
